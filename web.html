INTRODUCTION TO SJCHS

St John College of Humanities and Sciences (SJCHS) is in Palghar. SJCHS was inaugurated and blessed by Rt. Rev Dr Henry D’Souza – Bishop of Bellary Diocese on August 3, 2015.

The educational facilities provided in the rural area will offer professional and technical education to less fortunate students also, enabling them to take up brilliant careers.
